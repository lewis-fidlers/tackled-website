Built for how your brain actually works

Four small tools, each doing exactly one job.

⚡

Capture anywhere

⌥Space opens a quick-capture card over whatever you're doing. Type it, hit enter, forget it — Tackled remembers so you don't have to.

⌥ SPACE
🎯

Focus mode

One task, full screen, nothing else. It opens on the task you've been avoiding longest — restart what you're avoiding.

💭

Brain farts

Stray thoughts aren't tasks. Park them in their own inbox, search them later, and get back to what you were doing.

🌙

Calm mode

Tackled celebrates wins — confetti, sound, a little sparkle. If that's not what you need today, dial it back with one tap.
