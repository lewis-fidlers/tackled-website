Support

Here to help.

Get in touch

Something broken, confusing, or missing? Email lewisfidlers@gmail.com and you'll hear back directly — Tackled is a one-person project, so you're talking straight to the person who built it.

Where is my data stored?

Everything lives in a local SQLite database in your Mac's Application Support folder. Nothing syncs to a server today — see the privacy policy for details.

How do I back up my tasks?

Use the app menu → Export Data… to save a JSON copy of everything (tasks, notes, and reminders) anywhere on your Mac.

Notifications aren't showing up

Open System Settings → Notifications → Tackled and make sure alerts are allowed. You can also try the app's menu → Send Test Notification to confirm delivery.

The global capture shortcut (⌥Space) isn't working

Another app may already be using that shortcut. Quit Tackled, check your other menu-bar apps for a conflicting ⌥Space binding, then relaunch.

Feature requests

Always welcome — send them to the email above with a quick note on what you're trying to do.

Tackled runs entirely on your Mac. No account, no login, no server — your data never leaves your machine.
